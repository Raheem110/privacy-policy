# AI BG Remover

**AI BG Remover** is an Android app  **Developed by AR Infinity Studio** that lets you remove, replace, and edit image backgrounds directly on your device. Your photos are processed locally for background removal, helping protect your privacy.

---

## Features

* ✨ AI-powered background removal
* 🎨 Replace backgrounds with colors, gradients, or your own images
* 🌫️ Adjustable background blur
* ✂️ Crop, rotate, flip, erase, and restore tools
* 💾 Save images in JPG, PNG, or WEBP format
* 📤 Share your edited images with other apps
* 🌙 Light and Dark themes
* 🔒 Privacy-focused on-device image processing
