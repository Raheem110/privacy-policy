Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AI BG Remover is published by Raheem Tech. This policy explains what information the app processes, what leaves your device, and your choices.

1. Summary

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • Background removal, blur, and most editing run locally on your phone.
  • Your own photos are not uploaded to us for background removal.
  • The free version shows ads through Google AdMob.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information we do not collect on our servers

We do not operate user accounts and we do not receive or store on our servers:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Your photo library or camera images for background removal
  • Edited images, unless you choose to share them outside the app yourself

3. Device permissions

The app requests permissions only to provide features you choose to use:

  • Camera — capture a photo for editing when you tap Camera
  • Photos/Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / gallery access) — pick an image when you tap Gallery
  • Internet — load ads and request ad consent where required
  • Network state — check connectivity before ad requests

4. On-device processing

When you remove or blur a background, crop, erase, or otherwise edit an image, processing happens on your device using the built-in AI model. Those operations do not send your photos to Raheem Tech or to a background-removal server operated by us.

5. Diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics)

To improve stability, the app may send crash and error reports to Google Firebase Crashlytics. These reports may include device model, operating system version, app version, and technical stack traces. They do not include your photos or edited images. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Advertising and consent (Google AdMob)

This app may show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, diagnostic information, and similar data to deliver, measure, and personalize ads where permitted.

Where required by law, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for your consent before loading personalized ads. You can change ad personalization choices later in your device or Google account settings.

Google advertising and partner information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Background colors and gradients

When you choose a solid color, gradient, or transparent background after removing the background, that styling is generated entirely on your device. Your color choices are not uploaded to Raheem Tech.

8. In-app purchases (VIP)

Optional VIP is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Google Play. Google may collect payment and purchase information under its own policies. We do not receive your payment card details. Restore purchases uses your Google Play account on this device.

Google Play terms: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/

9. Storage, backups, and saving

Edited images are saved to your device only when you tap Save. We do not keep copies on our servers. Android backup or cloud backup behavior, if enabled on your device, is controlled by your device manufacturer and Google account settings.

10. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Your choices

  • You can deny or revoke permissions in Android Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.
  • You can manage ad personalization through Google/device settings and UMP consent choices where available.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The in-app Privacy policy screen shows the latest version.
